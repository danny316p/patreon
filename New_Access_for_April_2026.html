New Access for April 2026

This was another busy month marked by Billund trip planning and finalizing my portion of ColonialLUG's collaborative Beach Town layout. There were a few previews for each tier, but we'll focus on new albums added April 30th for the rest of this post.
  • Everyone now has access to a selection of photos from my 2019 visit to the Teddy Bear Art Museum in Billund, Denmark. These albums include: Teddy Bear Art Museum Tableau Exhibition (531 photos), Teddy Bear Art Museum Artist Teddy Bears (464 photos), Teddy Bear Art Museum VanderBear Exhibition (628 photos), Teddy Bear Art Museum Historical Exhibition (42 photos), and the temporary Teddy Bear Art Museum Steiff Exhibition (280 photos). An additional 6 albums (yes, LEGO fans, including the displays about the history of the house and the Kirk Kristiansens) and some unsorted photos have been held back for now.

    I also deployed a new sitemap-style webapp for more easily navigating my photos, complete with hierarchal- and filter-based views based on Collections. This responsive webapp works well on all screen sizes, effectively enabling Collections view on mobile (which Flickr officially does not support). You can easily find this tool from the About page of my Flickr profile.

    Everyone can also now see 12 360° photos from Maker Faire Philadelphia 2026. These will be sorted further once I add regular photos from that event, although I have categorized the 3 that show more of my MOCs and ColonialLUG's exhibit (I am partial to the view inside my hotel). This is all in addition to the final build snapshots of my part of ColonialLUG's collaborative Beach Town layout.
  • Everyone on the Discord server can now access the private version of the webapp mentioned above. This version uses the Flickr API to pull albums and collections as I see them when logged in. You can think of the extra album titles and thumbnails as a security leak, but I've decided that I'm OK with sharing this with my community.
  • Subscribers now have PREVIEW access to 657 photos of the LEGOLAND Billund Castle Hotel in 2019. These should be made public at some point in the future, but I have barely had a chance to comb through them myself yet - a motivating factor for uploading these now was to allow me to check during my upcoming trip on what I had already covered.
  • Members now have access to 43 photos of the DFDS Jubilee Seaways Ferry. This record-breaking build was dismantled outside of Skaerbaek Fan Weekend 2019, with many fans (myself included) claiming some of the parts and proceeds donated to Fairy Bricks. All 43 of my photos are from
  • Directors now have PREVIEW access to 4,512 unsorted photos of LEGOLAND Billund in 2019. These will be made public after they've been sorted into albums for various regions of the park, but will be exclusive to Directors for at least a few months.
  • ...and don't forget that you (everyone, for free) can follow my progress on build projects and organizing various collectibles, memorabilia, and historical items. You can follow me on Flickr and Patreon and join my Discord community for regular updates, with additional goodies shared with members on the various tiers.
Please note that the photo/video links in this post require that you are already logged in to Flickr with the correct permissions. Deep links with guest passes will be available on each tier's Discord -media channel. If you were involved in an event that I took photos of and would like to see pictures of your work, please reach out and I will try to grant you appropriate access free of charge. Patreon supporters at the higher tiers also have access to perks from lower tiers.
